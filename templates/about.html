About QuantiFlex

OUR MISSION

At UTBiome, we aim to put patients first to clinicians by providing cutting-edge rehabilitation technology that enhances recovery outcomes through real-time data and analytics.

OUR TECHNOLOGY

We use a dual-material construction of a polyester waffle knit outer layer and an elastomeric rib knit inner layer, permitting lateral stretch while restricting deformation in the vertical axis. We also implemented 3D printed TPU lateral supports for varus and valgus stability, alongside a neoprene foam patella support.

MEET THE TEAM


Our multidisciplinary team of engineers, life-science students, and researchers is dedicated to advancing knee rehabilitation through innovative solutions that empower both patients and healthcare providers. The UTBIOME team consists of experts in biology and physiology, software engineers, and experts in data analytics.
